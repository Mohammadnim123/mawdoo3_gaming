Privacy Policy

{{ site_name }} lets you turn ideas into playable browser games. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect and why.

What we store

Your account (email, handle, display name), the games you create and their versions, and engagement you produce (plays, likes, comments, follows). Generated games are served from an isolated, sandboxed origin.

Prompts & generation

Prompts you submit are sent to our generation service to build your game. We keep them associated with your game for reproducibility and editing.

Your choices

You control the visibility of each game (public, unlisted, private) and can delete your games. Contact us to remove your account.

This is a product policy for the platform, not legal advice.
